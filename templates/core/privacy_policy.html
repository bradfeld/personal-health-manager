Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Personal Health Manager ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our application.

This is a demo app so we make no guarantees about the security of the data. Please do not use this app if you have any concerns about the security of your data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and authentication information.
  • Health and Fitness Data: With your permission, we collect health and fitness data from third-party services like Whoop and Strava, including:
    • Activity data (duration, distance, calories burned, etc.)
    • Health metrics (heart rate, sleep data, recovery scores, etc.)
    • Performance statistics
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our application, including access times and pages viewed.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To display your health and fitness data in a meaningful way
  • To analyze trends and improve our application
  • To communicate with you about your account or our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Whoop: We access your Whoop data through their API with your permission. Please review Whoop's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.
  • Strava: We access your Strava data through their API with your permission. Please review Strava's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as required by law. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
